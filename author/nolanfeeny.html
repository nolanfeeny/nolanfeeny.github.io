Pre Study Abroad

 Tue 29 May 2018 by nolanfeeny

This summer I have plans to study abroad in Berlin, Germany from July 7 - August 18. I'll be taking the following two weeks to travel the rest of Europe with my brother, and will be back on August 30. I'll be taking two classes: a renewable simulation energy lab and a German language course. Hopefully I'll be more fluent halfway through the trip.

I'll try to post updates and pictures here, which will be good for me keeping my "coding" skills fresh. I might post the links to this website as well on facebook whenever I do update, so be on the lookout for that if you're interested. Or don't. It's a free country. Unlike Europe. Haha jokes, in reality I'm very excited to get more experience of culture outside of the U.S. Okay bye.
